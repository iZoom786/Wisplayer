The Data Flow in Action: A Real-Time Example

Let's trace the journey of a single piece of data, such as a user click on a website, as it flows through a concrete implementation of our open-source platform to provide immediate analytical value.

1. Event Source

User click events from a web application.

2. Ingestion: Kafka

Events are published to a Kafka topic for durable, high-throughput queuing.

3. Processing: Flink

A Flink job consumes the stream, performing real-time aggregations (e.g., clicks per minute).

4. Storage: ClickHouse

The aggregated results are written to ClickHouse, a fast analytical database.

5. Visualization: Superset

A dashboard in Apache Superset queries ClickHouse to display the results in near real-time.
